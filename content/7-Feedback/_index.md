I sincerely thank the organizers of the First Cloud Journey (FCJ) program and the mentors for giving me the opportunity to experience such a meaningful internship. Below are my personal feedback and suggestions after participating in the program.

### General Evaluation

**1. Working environment** The learning and working environment at FCJ is friendly, dynamic, and open. Members of the program were always willing to support me and answer questions whenever I faced difficulties while doing labs or implementing the project. The workspace and support platforms were organized neatly and systematically, helping interns focus on studying and practicing. To make the program even more connected, I suggest that the organizers add a few short online or offline team-bonding sessions so that members from different groups can get to know one another better.

**2. Mentor and admin team support** The mentors provided very detailed guidance, explained new technology concepts clearly, and always encouraged interns to ask questions proactively. The admin team responded quickly to procedural matters, provided complete documents, and created the most favorable conditions for interns. What I appreciated most was the mentors' guidance method: instead of giving answers immediately, they often guided me with questions so I could explore, think independently, and solve technical issues by myself. This helped me grow very quickly.

**3. Relevance between work and academic major** As an Information Technology student, the internship content at FCJ matched and strongly supported my academic direction. The real project about a high-load system and the knowledge of the AWS ecosystem helped me turn the theory I learned at university into practical infrastructure deployment scenarios.

**4. Learning opportunities and skill development** The internship helped me make significant progress in both technical and soft skills. Technically, I gained deeper exposure to Cloud architecture, the operation of large language models (LLMs), and Prompt Engineering. In terms of soft skills, I practiced business analysis thinking (BA), load testing scenario design (QA/QC), time management, and professional communication when collaborating in a team.

**5. Culture and teamwork spirit** The culture at FCJ is positive and professional: everyone respects differences, works seriously, and is still willing to share and support one another. When facing difficult labs or urgent project deadlines, the teamwork spirit became even stronger, with members actively helping each other to achieve the team's shared goals.

**6. Policies and benefits for interns** The program has clear support policies and offers flexible scheduling, allowing me to balance university study with the internship. In particular, the opportunity to attend in-depth internal training sessions and access high-quality AWS technology materials was an extremely valuable benefit for students.

---

### Survey Answers

* **What were you most satisfied with during the internship?** What satisfied me most was the opportunity to participate in major technology events such as the "Automated Prompt Engineering" Workshop and "AWS Vietnam Community Day 2026". These experiences allowed me to learn directly from industry experts, broaden my perspective on modern technology trends, and better understand how large organizations apply Cloud and AI in real scenarios.
* **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?** The program contains a large and advanced amount of knowledge, especially in AWS infrastructure and system optimization. Therefore, the organizers could extend the initial prerequisite preparation period slightly or provide basic sample labs in advance so that future students do not feel overwhelmed at the beginning.
* **If you introduced this program to friends, would you recommend it? Why?** I would definitely recommend my friends to join this program. It is a practical internship environment where students do not simply do administrative tasks but are truly immersed in core technology projects, work with real AWS Cloud infrastructure, and receive clear career orientation.

---

### Suggestions and Future Expectations

* **Suggestions to improve the experience:** I hope the organizers will continue maintaining and expanding Tech Talk sessions or hands-on specialized Workshops like those in this internship, because they bring very high practical value to students.
* **Future expectation:** I hope to have the opportunity to continue accompanying the First Cloud Journey program in the next stages, or to contribute to the community as a supporter for future intern cohorts.
* **Other feedback:** I would like to send my best wishes and deepest thanks to the Admin team and mentors of First Cloud Journey. I wish the program continued growth and hope it remains a strong launchpad for many generations of Information Technology students in the future.
